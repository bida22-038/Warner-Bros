THE FLASH
The world's colliding when the Flash is using his superpower to travel in time, changing events of the past. But he becomes trapped in reality where General Zod is returning, threatening annihilation, when his attempt to save his family inadvertently changes the future. With no other superheroes to turn to, the Flash looks to coax a very different Batman out of retirement and rescue an imprisoned Kryptonian -- albeit not the one he's looking for.
