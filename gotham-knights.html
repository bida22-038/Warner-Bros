GOTHAM KNIGHTS
Batman's adopted son, Turner Hayes is framed for his killing. With the charismatic and hard-charging District Attorney Harvey Dent and the GCPD hot on his trail, Turner must rely on allies including his best friend, Stephanie Brown, and unlikely Batman sidekick Carrie Kelley. But soon they're going to find out that in Gotham City there is a bigger and darker force at work. To become Gotham's next generation of saviors known as the Gotham Knights, this mismatched fugitive team must come together.
