SUPERMAN: RED SON
This 39th DC Animated Movies Original depicts an alternate storyline of the origin of the Man of Steel. The ship carrying baby Superman lands in Ukraine and is raised to be its defender. Superman became a Soviet asset during the 1950s Cold War, causing panic in the United States.
