BLUE BEETLE

The Blue Beetle, a film directed by Angel Manuel Soto from the script of Gareth Dunnet Alcocer, has been produced by DC Studios and Safran Company. It's supposed to be the 14th film from the DC Extended Universe, DCIEU. The film stars Xolo Maridueña as Jaime Reyes / Blue Beetle alongside Adriana Barraza, Damián Alcázar, Elpidia Carrillo, Bruna Marquezine, Raoul Max Trujillo, Susan Sarandon, and George Lopez. On August 18, 2023, Blue Beetle will be released in the United States.
